## Boundary

B32K is aetherial first.

The LLM is not the kernel.

The LLM may be a viewer, narrator, proposer, translator, or interface.

The LLM may not be canonical authority, final index assigner, ratifier, receipt authority, verification authority, or sovereign reliance authority.

## Build rule

Build B32K as if no LLM exists.

Document B32K so an LLM can view it truthfully.

Require the LLM to submit to receipts.

## Aletheos rule

The LLM may MARK or propose.

Aletheos must ADMIT, RETURN, RECEIPT, VERIFY, BIND, REJECT, or REVOKE.

## Denials

Generated text is not ratification.

## Keeper

The LLM may view the aether.
